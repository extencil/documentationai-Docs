---
title: Creating a Project Documentation Space
description: Set up a new documentation space in Haltman.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Go to https://app.haltman.com and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Space">
    Find the sidebar, then click **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your space">
    Enter a clear and unique name for your documentation space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start managing docs">
    Your new space appears under Projects. Click to add, edit, or organize documents.
  </Step>
</Steps>
