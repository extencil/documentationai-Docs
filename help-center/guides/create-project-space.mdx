## Create your first alias

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to forward.haltman.io" icon="globe">
    Visit [https://forward.haltman.io/](https://forward.haltman.io/) in your browser.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your alias" icon="edit-3">
    Pick a **handle** (local-part), select an available **domain**, and enter your **destination email**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm via email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the confirmation link. Click it to activate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test it" icon="check-circle">
    Send a test email to your new alias and verify it arrives at your destination inbox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
