---
title: Add Your Domain
description: How to add your own root domain to the free mail forwarding service.
---
Follow these steps:

1. Find the interactive menu on homepage:

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-3a9a3d5c-3821-4759-aa2d-02faf765c2c5/doc-bd7c5e4b-3e9c-4753-ac40-16ace83257b9/1771789489193-563xhk8inr5-pasted-image-1771789486673.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="895" height="761" alt="" />

1) Type your own FQDN:

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-3a9a3d5c-3821-4759-aa2d-02faf765c2c5/doc-bd7c5e4b-3e9c-4753-ac40-16ace83257b9/1771789449002-k4i3ie74pyj-pasted-image-1771789444870.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="895" height="761" alt="" />

2) It will show you required DNS entries & start automatic polling every X seconds to identity any DNS updates:&#x20;

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-3a9a3d5c-3821-4759-aa2d-02faf765c2c5/doc-bd7c5e4b-3e9c-4753-ac40-16ace83257b9/1771789784573-f5em49kwvap-pasted-image-1771789782838.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1071" height="879" alt="" />

3) System will show the validated DNS entries:

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-3a9a3d5c-3821-4759-aa2d-02faf765c2c5/doc-bd7c5e4b-3e9c-4753-ac40-16ace83257b9/1771790023397-0i9ypppkxocr-pasted-image-1771790015760.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1071" height="879" alt="" />

4) When all DNS entries got validated, it will be displayed as ACTIVE:

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-3a9a3d5c-3821-4759-aa2d-02faf765c2c5/doc-bd7c5e4b-3e9c-4753-ac40-16ace83257b9/1771790108492-p2bcb72zqe-pasted-image-1771790106277.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1071" height="879" alt="" />

5) Just reload the page and you will find your domain listed under domains menu:

   <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-3a9a3d5c-3821-4759-aa2d-02faf765c2c5/doc-bd7c5e4b-3e9c-4753-ac40-16ace83257b9/1771790176716-c7nnz7wf63-pasted-image-1771790174430.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1071" height="879" alt="" />
