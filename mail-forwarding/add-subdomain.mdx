<Callout kind="alert">
  This page is exclusively for those who have a subdomain and want to add it to the Free Mail Forwarding Service at [forward.haltman.io](https://forward.haltman.io).
</Callout>

## Important

This is an **asynchronous process**. To add a domain or subdomain, please contact us through the channels below. We will provide all the support you need to configure your DNS records correctly.

- [root@haltman.io](mailto:root@haltman.io)
- [https://t.me/haltman_group](https://t.me/haltman_group)

## Required DNS Configurations â€” Subdomain

**Example subdomain:** `sub.example.com`

### DNS Records Table

| Type | Name | Content | Priority |
|------|------|---------|----------|
| MX | sub | mail.abin.lat | 10 |
| TXT | sub | `v=spf1 mx -all` | N/A |
| TXT | \_dmarc.sub | `v=DMARC1; p=none` | N/A |

### Zone File (Copy & Paste)

```bash
sub.example.com.              MX   10 mail.abin.lat.
sub.example.com.              TXT     "v=spf1 mx -all"
_dmarc.sub.example.com.       TXT     "v=DMARC1; p=none"
```

<Callout kind="tip">
  For root domain configuration, see [Add Your Domain](/mail-forwarding/add-domain).
</Callout>
