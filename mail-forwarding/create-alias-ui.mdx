## Create your alias via the web UI

Access [https://forward.haltman.io/](https://forward.haltman.io/) to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request your alias" icon="edit-3">
    Fill in the following fields:

    1. Select your **handle** (`handle`@domain)
    2. Select the **alias domain** (handle@`domain`)
    3. Enter your **destination email** address

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1768366425180/a341dfca-ac97-41c8-9d39-a08de676f51a.png" alt="Create alias form" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your email (anti-abuse)" icon="mail">
    A confirmation email is sent to your destination address. Click the confirmation link to activate the alias.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1768366480552/cb532020-7333-4a5c-a801-9505c8342e23.png" alt="Confirmation email sent" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1768366676148/69a5b60e-fb57-46af-881b-13e944382a61.png" alt="Email confirmed" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test your new alias" icon="check-circle">
    Send a test email to your new alias and verify it arrives at your destination inbox.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1768366968034/f1f5bf4d-d5bb-4b93-bd8c-7ef8b5607756.png" alt="Test email received" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
