## What is Mail Forwarding?

The free mail forwarding service at [forward.haltman.io](https://forward.haltman.io) lets you create **email aliases** that forward messages to your real inbox. No account or password required â€” just confirm ownership of your destination email.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your alias" icon="edit-3">
    Pick a handle (e.g., `myname`) and select an available domain (e.g., `fwd.haltman.io`). Your alias becomes `myname@fwd.haltman.io`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your destination" icon="mail">
    Provide the real email address where forwarded messages should be delivered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm via email" icon="check-circle">
    A confirmation link is sent to your destination email (anti-abuse). Click it to activate the alias.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start receiving" icon="inbox">
    Any email sent to your alias is forwarded to your real inbox immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
