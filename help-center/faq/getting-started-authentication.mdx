## Signing In

Access Haltman at `https://app.haltman.com`.

1. Click **Sign In** on the homepage.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Use your password or "Sign in with Google".
4. Successful login redirects you to your dashboard.

## Reset Password

If you forgot your password, click **Forgot Password?** and follow the instructions.

## Notes
- Email verification may be required on first login.
